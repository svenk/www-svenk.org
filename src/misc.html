Miscellaneous

This page lists various activities around the web and in real life where you can find me occasionally.

technikum29 Computer Museum

I operate the private technikum museum for computer and communication history. Within the hobbyist vintage computing community, it takes a distinct position thanks to its one-of-a-kind dedicated building, allowing it to offer a home to extremely heavy vintage computers such as vacuum-tube computers, punch card systems and mainframes. Since 2019, together with a small team of highly motivated volunteers, we shape this heritage.

The museum is located near Frankfurt, Germany. We have about 40 public events per year hosted in the museum and carried out by my fellows.
